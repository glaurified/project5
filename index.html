Cozy Fall Vegan Recipes

Although I myself am not vegan, I personally enjoy cooking meat-and-dairy-free because it is more accessible to the people in my life, and I've found that it can be also be a more creative and affordable way to cook.

Below are three recipe tutorials for a full cozy fall meal: a beverage (with several options), an appetizer/side dish (a warm butternut squash soup), and a main dish (pumpkin pasta with spinach and sundried tomatoes), all relatively simple to make and vegan-friendly.

Beverages

I like how this video gives you a good handful of fall-themed drink options to choose from. The recipes that most interested me were the Pumpkin Spice Chai Latte (#1) and the Homemade Apple Cider (#7). Both contain delicious fall spices and are suitable for serving either warm or iced.


Video by Mina Rome.

Appetizer

One of my favorite fall-themed recipes to make is a roasted butternut squash soup. I improvise making my own, but this recipe is a pretty close approximation of my process. I usually chop and boil potatoes along with the squash before blending and add lots of lemon juice!


Video by Liv B.

Entree

Finally, I chose this pumpkin pasta recipe for the main dish in my autumnal menu. This recipe also calls for sundried tomatoes and spinach, both of which I love to include in a pasta dish. If I was feeling extra fancy, I would probably add some fried shittake mushrooms in with the sauce as well.


Video by 'Make it Dairy Free'.
